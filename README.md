## Why This Handbook?

In my experience mentoring teams and leading technical projects, I've realized that the hardest part of software development isn't the syntaxâ€”it's the **mindset**.

This handbook serves as:
1.  **A Knowledge Base:** A reference for SOLID principles, Design Patterns, and Architecture.
2.  **A Communication Tool:** A shared standard to align teams on "how we do things here."
3.  **A Manifesto:** A plea for simplicity in an increasingly complex tech landscape.

> *"Hazlo simple o no lo hagas."* (Keep it simple or don't do it.)
